About Earthy Brews

Our story, our values, and the people behind your favorite coffee

Our Story

How Earthy Brews came to be

At Earthy Brews, we believe in the power of a perfect cup of coffee to transform your day. Our journey began in 2015 with a simple passion for quality coffee and a dream to create a space where people could connect, relax, and enjoy life's simple pleasures.

Founded by two friends who shared a love for exceptional coffee, Earthy Brews started as a small cart at the local farmers' market. The overwhelming response from the community encouraged us to expand, and within a year, we opened our first brick-and-mortar location.

What sets us apart is our commitment to quality and sustainability. We source our beans directly from small-scale farmers around the world, ensuring fair compensation and environmentally responsible farming practices. Our beans are roasted in small batches to bring out their unique flavors and aromas.

Over the years, we've grown from a single location to multiple cafés across the city, but our mission remains the same: to serve exceptional coffee in a warm, welcoming environment that feels like home.

Today, Earthy Brews is more than just a coffee shop—it's a community hub where ideas are exchanged, friendships are formed, and memories are created over the perfect cup of coffee.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Sustainability

We're committed to environmentally responsible practices, from bean to cup. We use compostable packaging, minimize waste, and support sustainable farming.

Fair Trade

We believe in ethical sourcing and fair compensation for farmers. Our direct trade relationships ensure that the people who grow our coffee are treated with respect and dignity.

Quality

We never compromise on quality. From selecting the finest beans to training our baristas in the art of coffee preparation, excellence is our standard.

Community

We believe in creating spaces where people can connect and belong. We support local initiatives and strive to be a positive force in our community.
