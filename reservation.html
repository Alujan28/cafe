Reservation Policy

  • Reservations are recommended for groups of 4 or more.
  • We hold reservations for 15 minutes past the scheduled time.
  • For cancellations, please notify us at least 2 hours in advance.
  • For private events or large groups (10+), please contact us directly at (555) 123-4567.
  • Special dietary requirements can be accommodated with advance notice.

Private Events

Looking to host a private event? Earthy Brews offers a cozy and inviting space for various occasions:

  • Business meetings and presentations
  • Birthday celebrations
  • Book clubs and study groups
  • Coffee tastings and workshops
  • Small gatherings and reunions

For private event inquiries, please contact us at events@earthybrews.com or call (555) 123-4567.
